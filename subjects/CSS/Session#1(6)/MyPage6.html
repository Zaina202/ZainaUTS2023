Isra' Software & Computer Co

Isra Software and Computer Company was established in 1997 in Nablus as a specialized in the prodection of software at Palestine ans Arab Countries.

Includes more than 50 employees distributed in the following main departments (Research and Development, Training and Operation, Marketing and Sales, Finance, Audit and Quality).

In addition to the company's headquarters in the city of Nablus, the company's branch was opened in Ramallah in 2004 to serve and fllow-up customers in the central and southern region.
